The Oak & Stone Story

Nestled in the heart of Dublin, Oak & Stone Carpentry was founded on a simple principle: to create woodwork that is as enduring as it is beautiful. Our passion lies in blending the rich heritage of traditional Irish carpentry with sleek, contemporary design.

From our initial consultation to the final polish, every project is a collaborative journey. We listen intently to your vision, offering expert guidance to ensure the final product not only meets but exceeds your expectations. Our commitment is to quality, precision, and a finish that speaks for itself.

We are proud to serve Dublin's discerning homeowners, innovative property developers, and leading interior designers who share our appreciation for exceptional craftsmanship.
